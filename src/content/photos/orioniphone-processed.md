A wide-field view of the Orion constellation captured and processed from an iPhone. Even with a phone camera, the belt stars, Betelgeuse's orange tint, and a hint of the nebulosity around M42 can come through with the right processing. It's a reminder that astrophotography spans every budget — from phone snapshots under dark skies to hours of cooled-camera integration from the back garden.
